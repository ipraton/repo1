Quiz 4a

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
\item Here is a table showing the values of a function $f$, along with values of its derivative $f'$.

\qquad \begin{tabular}{l|ccc} \hline
$x$ & 0 & 1 & 2 \\ \hline
$f(x)$ & 0 & 2 & 2\\
$f'(x)$ & 1 & 0 & $-1$\\ \hline
\end{tabular}

Let $g(x)=(x^2+1)f(x)$. Find $g'(1)$.

\vspace{3.5in}
\item Let $h(x)=2e^x - 3f(x)$, where $f(x)$ is the function above. Find the equation of the tangent line to the curve $y=h(x)$ at $x=0$. 
\end{enumerate}
